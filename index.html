About Me

I am Nico Alvarez, a software developer and an ethical Web 3.0 enthusiast who loves nature, family and creative hobbies. I regard simplicity, flexibility and honesty. My life and that of my family is based upon creationistic and exetic values that strive for peace, trust, understanding for each other and mutual agape. Our generation is being faced with many challenges and I firmly believe that restructruring the current social and economic system on the basis of the values I defend is of key importance. I shall do whatever is necessary to make of this world a better place, especially if we define "better" as more sustainable, efficient and where the inhabitants of this world can peacefully and contently interact with each other.
